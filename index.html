Best Playlists I've Made So Far

Continuing the now prevalent tradition of revealing the top 4 films as posted on letterboxd, I have the pleasure to anounce my top 4 playlists I've created on Spotify and why I think you should give them a chance using 4 criteria and rating them from 1 to 10 (according to me because had no time to ask my friends).

CRITERIA:

  • Nostalgia Factor: The playlist is able to transport back to a specific moment in life.
  • Emotional Conection: The playlist's potential to evoke feelings or be associated with specific mood or emotions.
  • Versatility: The playlist is good enough on its own to be shared in public to people I'm not familiar with.
  • Cultural and Generational Identity (CGI): Related to growing up in Colombia during the 00s.



NOW, WITHOUT FURTHER ADO...


TOP 4 Best Playlists I've Made So Far

  1. Chucu chucu (navidad en marzo)

    Chucu chucu is a sound manifestation that has hints of cumbia and tropical music from the center of the Colombia, its history has been marked by artists who have made several generations dance at family parties.These playlist includes songs that are part of the repertoire we usually listen to during the holidays in December.

    • Nostalgia Factor: 9/10
    • Emotional Connection: 7/10
    • Versatility: 7/10
    • CGI: 10/10

    TOTAL SCORE: 8/10

    Listen here.

  2. Canciones de amor para dedicar

    Canciones de amor para dedicar (Love Songs to Dedicate) is a versatile playlist including songs in Spanish and English in a wide veriety of genres, but mainly pop, indie rock, and baladas. It includes some of the songs previous partners and relationships have shared with me and that to this day still move me deeply.

    • Nostalgia Factor: 10/10
    • Emotional Connection: 10/10
    • Versatility: 8/10
    • CGI: 4/10

    TOTAL SCORE: 8/10

    Listen here.

  3. Musiquita para planchar parchadito

    Mũsica para planchar (Music to listen to while ironing clothes) is not a music genre itself but a selection of romantic songs or latin ballads made during the 70-90s. It includes songs by Amanda Miguel, Selena, Luis Miguel, Camilo Sesto, Yuri and Jeanette among others. Although many spotify playlists were created recently including these songs, these are my personal favorites and I always listen to them while painting, doing printmaking or whenever I go to karaoke.

    • Nostalgia Factor: 10/10
    • Emotional Connection: 8/10
    • Versatility: 7/10
    • CGI: 9/10

    TOTAL SCORE: 8/10

    Listen here.

  4. GIRLS, all I really want is girls

    Pop, Rock, Indie and Latin music from the 90s and 2000s. This playlist is a celebration of girl power and includes songs from artists like Shakira, Lady Gaga, Madonna, Rihanna, Britney Spears, and many more. Perfect for a girls night out or a night in with besties.

    • Nostalgia Factor: 10/10
    • Emotional Connection: 8/10
    • Versatility: 10/10
    • CGI: 9/10

    TOTAL SCORE: 9/10

    Listen here.
